Place your custom images and audio here.

Photos (gallery):
- Replace URLs in index.html with local paths, e.g. assets/photo-1.jpg

Background music:
1. Add your file, e.g. assets/wedding-music.mp3
2. In script.js set: const MUSIC_SRC = "assets/wedding-music.mp3";

Floating flower PNGs:
- Decorative images are loaded from a public URL in index.html.
- For offline use, save PNGs into /assets/ and update the img src paths.

Wedding date / countdown:
- Edit WEDDING_DATE at the top of script.js (must match your event copy in index.html).
